# Quick Start

Get your CV analysed in under a minute.

## 1. Visit ReviewCV

Go to [review.cv](https://review.cv) in your browser.

## 2. Upload Your CV

Drag and drop your CV file onto the upload area, or click to browse your files. Supported formats:

- **PDF** (recommended)
- **DOCX** (Microsoft Word)
- **DOC** (Legacy Word format)

## 3. Review Your Results

Once uploaded, ReviewCV analyses your CV and presents:

- **Overall Score** — A summary rating of your CV's effectiveness
- **Category Breakdown** — Scores for formatting, content quality, ATS compatibility, and more
- **Suggestions** — Specific improvements ranked by impact

## 4. Improve and Re-upload

Make the suggested changes to your CV and upload again to see your improved score.
