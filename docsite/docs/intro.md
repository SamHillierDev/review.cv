# Introduction

ReviewCV is an AI-powered CV and resume analysis tool that helps job seekers improve their applications. Upload your CV and receive instant, actionable feedback on formatting, content, and overall effectiveness.

## What ReviewCV Does

- **Analyses your CV** — Scans formatting, structure, and content for common issues
- **Scores key areas** — Rates your CV across categories like clarity, impact, and ATS compatibility
- **Provides suggestions** — Offers specific, actionable improvements you can make right away
- **Supports multiple formats** — Works with PDF, DOCX, and DOC files
